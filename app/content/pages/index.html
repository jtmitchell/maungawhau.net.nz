Small is Beautiful


We are a small company, able to be both flexible and agile in development.

Tools of the Trade

Maungawhau IT specialises in all things Unix and Linux. This leads naturally to webservers, e-commerce, and all things Internet.

For server backends we use and recommend Ubuntu, Python, MySQL, and the Amazon Cloud. Optionally you may prefer Red Hat, Perl, PHP, or PostgreSQL. Pick one of Apache, Cherokee, or nginx.

For development on the client-side, we are exploring AngularJS for both single-page apps, and to enhance interactivity for individual pages. These apps communicate with well-defined APIs on the traditional backend.

The new excitement for infrastructure is Cloud Foundry and Stackato for delivering PaaS clouds.
